The Ultimate Plug-In Hybrid for Families

America's most versatile, spacious, and kid-friendly plug-in hybrid, the 2022 Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid carries seven passengers anywhere with a segment competitive pure electric range for local drives and an efficient hybrid powertrain for longer road trips. Owners may qualify for up to $7,500 of the federal tax credit.

The Most Advanced Available All-Wheel-Drive System in its Class.

The 2022 Chrysler Pacifica brings peace of mind to busy parents with more standard safety and security features than any vehicle and the most advanced available All-Wheel-Drive (AWD) system in its class. Innovative seating and storage with SUV-beating cargo space, cabin-wide connectivity, and an available advanced rear-seat entertainment system help make Pacifica a favorite of active and growing families.

The Legendary Chrysler 300

Speed, strength and sophisticated design have made the Chrysler 300 an American icon. Go zero to 60 in under 6 seconds with the most powerful V8 engine in its class. Cruise confidently with Class-Exclusive available All-Wheel Drive (AWD) featuring a front axle disconnect system that seamlessly engages to hold traction in a variety of driving conditions. And wherever the 2022 Chrysler 300 takes you and your crew, always show up in style.
