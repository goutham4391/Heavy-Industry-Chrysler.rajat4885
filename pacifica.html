Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid

AMERICA'S ONLY PLUG-IN HYBRID MINIVAN

Stretch your dollar in the Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid, a seven-passenger Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) that starts under $47K. Touting an impressive 32-mile electric range combined with an outstanding total driving range of 520 miles, the Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid seamlessly switches between electric and gas, delivering ultimate fuel efficiency so you can leave range anxiety in the dust.

EXPLORE HYBRID

Performance

MOST ADVANCED AVAILABLE ALL-WHEEL DRIVE SYSTEM IN ITS CLASS

The Chrysler Pacifica offers the only fully automatic All-Wheel-Drive System in the segment capable of transferring 100% of the available engine torque to the rear wheels or whichever wheels have more available traction, so you can drive with confidence.

EXPLORE AWD & CAPABILITY

Technology

PERSONALIZED ENTERTAINMENT

Keep your pack entertained with the impressive Uconnect® 5 system on the largest standard touchscreen in its class. Access wireless-connected Apple CarPlay® support and Android Auto™ Compatibility, Amazon Alexa Voice Control and more. Store up to five unique user profiles to personalize your experience and get five times faster performance versus the previous generation with over-the-air updates, plus the ability to connect two smartphones simultaneously.

EXPLORE TECHNOLOGY
