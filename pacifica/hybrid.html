AMERICA'S ONLY PLUG-IN HYBRID MINIVAN

DON'T WORRY, DRIVE HAPPY.

Our system detects when it’s best to use electric or gas and seamlessly switches between the two separate driving modes. So, you can relieve your range anxiety and enjoy an efficient and impressive 520-mile total range.

CHARGE WITH EVERY STOP

Using the built-in regenerative braking feature, the Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid is capable of helping charge the battery when braking or stopping. When enabled in the "L" drive mode, the standard Max Regeneration cluster messaging icon appears to assure you of the increased system regeneration.

CHARGING MADE EASY

With a charge time up to six times faster than typical Level I charging options, the Chrysler - Brand Level II EV Home Charging Station allows you to spend less time waiting for your Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid to charge and more time to enjoy the drive. The easy-to-use, small, lightweight, lockable enclosure’s quick-release mount is weatherproof for indoor or outdoor installation.

Additionally, we’ve teamed up with Qmerit to make installing a faster charger more convenient. With their certified electrification technicians and top-rated customer care specialists, Qmerit will ensure your satisfaction at every step. For the smart-home minded, the charging unit includes built-in Wi-Fi connectivity to control charging anytime, anywhere via a mobile app and web portal. The smart grid optimization allows you to reduce energy costs by charging at low-rate times and participate in available programs to further lower the cost of owning and driving your Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid. YOU DESERVE SOME CREDIT

Not all hybrid vehicles still qualify for the onetime $7,500 federal tax credit. However, with the purchase of a Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid, you may be eligible. This nonrefundable credit doesn’t carry over year-to-year, so make sure you take advantage while you can. You can receive a portion of the $7,500 tax credit even if your tax liability is below $7,500.

EXAMPLE OF HOW YOUR TAX CREDIT CAN BE APPLIED

The below example assumes an individual’s annual income is $100,000 with an estimated annual federal tax liability of $17,000. By purchasing a new Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid, they could qualify for a one-time federal tax credit of up to $7,500.

This tax credit would reduce the buyer’s federal tax liability for the year in which the vehicle is purchased. $17,000 estimated annual tax liability - $7,500 federal tax credit = $9,500 new estimated annual federal tax liability.

Generally, federal taxes are paid throughout the year through withholding taxes from paychecks or through estimated payments. As a result, this tax credit and the related reduction to the federal tax liability could result in a higher federal tax refund when filing his/her federal tax return.

TAKE THE LONG WAY HOME

Offering electric or gas, you can kiss range anxiety goodbye in the Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid. The exceptional and efficient hybrid powertrain delivers a pure electric range for local driving and gas range for extended excursions, so you have the freedom to go wherever you want—near or far.

  • ROOMY ENOUGH FOR EVERYONE, OR ANYTHING

    The Chrysler Pacifica Hybrid offers more cargo space than a full-size SUV with 81 different seating configurations. The most family friendly seven-passenger plug-in hybrid lets you remove the second-row seats and even stow the third row into the floor to make more room for extra cargo.

TECH THAT PUTS YOU IN CONTROL

Track your vehicle’s performance with ease on the 10.1-inch color touchscreen and adjust your charging schedule to run during less expensive off-peak hours plus view your power flow, driving history and more.

  • KNOWLEDGE IS POWER

    Remotely obtain a driving history report, view your vehicle’s charging progress, locate a charging station and more with the hybrid app and Uconnect®—all from the palm of your hand.
