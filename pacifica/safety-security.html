MORE STANDARD SAFETY AND SECURITY FEATURES THAN ANY VEHICLE IN THE INDUSTRY

Standard Safety & Security Features
Standard Safety & Security Features
STAY AHEAD OF THE CURVE

The Chrysler Pacifica offers more standard safety and security features than any vehicle in the industry, so your family can stay safe and sound.

FULL-SPEED FORWARD COLLISION WARNING WITH ACTIVE BRAKING

Standard Full-Speed Forward Collision Warning with Active Braking assists you by providing both visual and audible warnings as well as applying Automated Emergency Braking to help bring the vehicle to a full stop, if needed.

PLAY VIDEO

ADAPTIVE CRUISE CONTROL WITH STOP AND GO

Standard Adaptive Cruise Control with Stop and Go (ACC+) uses sensors to help maintain your preset distance between your Chrysler Pacifica and the vehicle in front of it. ACC+ functions between 0 mph and highway cruising speeds.

PLAY VIDEO

PEDESTRIAN AUTOMATIC EMERGENCY BRAKING SYSTEM

Standard Pedestrian Automatic Emergency Braking will automatically brake for pedestrians when necessary.

PLAY VIDEO

LED HEADLAMPS AND TAILLAMPS

See it all with a standard LED lighting system that delivers optimal capability. The 100%-wider headlamp beam width and increased headlamp beam length offer superior night-time driving visibility over the previous design.

PLAY VIDEO

LANESENSE® LANE DEPARTURE WARNING WITH LANE
KEEP ASSIST

Standard LaneSense Lane Departure Warning with Lane Keep Assist automatically helps guide you back into your lane if youve drifted outside of it unintentionally.

PLAY VIDEO

BLIND SPOT MONITORING SYSTEM

The standard Blind Spot Monitoring System helps you make safer lane changes. When the system detects a vehicle entering one of your blind spot zones it will alert you by lighting an icon in the corresponding exterior mirror.

PLAY VIDEO

PARKSENSE REAR PARK ASSIST

The standard ParkSense® Rear Park Assist System provides visual and audible indications of the distance between the rear fascia and a detected obstacle when backing up. Brakes may be automatically applied and released when backing up if the system detects a possible collision.

PLAY VIDEO

REAR CROSS PATH DETECTION

Standard Rear Cross Path Detection aids you when backing out of parking spaces where your vision may be blocked. Proceed slowly from the parking space until the rear end of the vehicle is exposed. The Rear Cross Path Detection System will then alert you if any oncoming vehicle is detected.

PLAY VIDEO

THE AIR FILTRATION SYSTEM IS STANDARD ON THE ENTIRE LINEUP

We have enhanced the air filtration systems in our vehicles to provide even cleaner air for you and your family. The new system filters out 85% of air particulates, reducing many types of solid and liquid airborne mattersuch as dust, smoke, droplets and aerosolsmaking it the cleanest cabin air ever in a Chrysler Brand vehicle.

Advanced Safety & Security Features
Advanced Safety & Security Features
SAFETY FIRST

You can never be too safe. With the available 360 Surround View Camera, Parallel and Perpendicular Park Assist and ParkSense® Front Park Assist, your Chrysler Pacifica offers added assistance and protection when you need it most.

360 SURROUND VIEW CAMERA

Always be aware of your surroundings with the available 360 Surround View Camera that offers a 360 view around the entire vehicle with top and rear views to help avoid obstacles.

PLAY VIDEO

PARKSENSE® FRONT PARK ASSIST

The available ParkSense Front Park Assist System provides visual and audible indications of the distance between the front fascia and a detected obstacle when moving forward. Brakes may be automatically applied and released when pulling ahead if the system detects a possible collision.

PLAY VIDEO

PARALLEL AND PERPENDICULAR PARK ASSIST

With available Parallel and Perpendicular Park Assist, the Chrysler Pacifica helps you park by guiding the vehicle into those tough-to-navigate spots on the first try and park like a pro.
