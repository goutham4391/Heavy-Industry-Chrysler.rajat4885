LEARN MORE ABOUT SELECTING CABS AND BOXES

  • Regular Cab

    The Regular Cab seats the driver and up to two passengers and has maximum towing and payload capability.

  • QUAD CAB®

    Make any ride a spacious experience with seating for the driver and up to five passengers. Decent towing/payload power. Only available on Ram 1500.

  • MEGA CAB®

    Maximize the cab space with room for the driver and up to five passengers in the Mega Cab®. Carrying a full complement of passengers may influence payload/towing capacity. Only available on Heavy Duty trucks.

  • CREW CAB

    The Crew Cab gets the job done. This configuration has a roomy interior for a driver and up to five passengers, while still allowing for ample towing/payload capacity.

  • 5-foot 7-inch box

    With lower curb weight and slightly higher towing capability, this box not only has sufficient length but impressive power as well. Only available on Ram 1500 and Ram 1500 Classic.

  • 6-FOOT 4-INCH BOX

    The perfect box length for drivers looking for a compromise between maximum towing and maximum payload capability.

  • 8-FOOT BOX

    Command large projects with confidence. With an impressive 8-foot box length, this configuration boasts an equally large payload capacity.

  • CHASSIS CAB CAB-TO-AXLE LENGTH—REGULAR CAB

    With four cab-to-axle lengths available in 60-inch, 84-inch (3500/4500/5500), 108-inch and 120-inch (4500/5500), Ram Chassis Cab can accommodate any job with your choice of upfit.

  • CHASSIS CAB CAB-TO-AXLE LENGTH—CREW CAB

    With two cab-to-axle lengths available in 60-inch and 84-inch (3500/4500/5500), Ram Chassis Cab can take on the toughest jobs with your upfit of choice.

LEARN MORE ABOUT SELECTING CARGO LENGTHS AND CARGO HEIGHTS
 

  • RAM PROMASTER CITY® CARGO LENGTHS

    The Ram ProMaster City® offers a Cargo Van length (7-foot 3-inch) and a Wagon cargo length (4-foot 6-inch) with the rear seats in place and a cargo length (5-foot 9-inch) with the rear seat folded—perfect for the next job.

  • RAM PROMASTER® CARGO LENGTHS

    With four cargo lengths (8-foot, 10-foot, 12-foot, or 13-foot) to choose from, the Ram ProMaster® offers configurations to fit your task.

  • RAM PROMASTER® CARGO HEIGHTS

    With three cargo heights to choose from (66-inch, 77-inch, or 86-inch), the Ram ProMaster® offers the configurations you need to get your job done.

  • RAM PROMASTER CITY® CARGO HEIGHTS

    The Ram ProMaster City® offers two cargo heights (48.7-inch or 51.8-inch) to choose from.
